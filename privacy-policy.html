This Privacy Policy explains how EiferOne Ltd ("EiferOne", "we", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information about you when you visit our website, contact us, or engage our services.

03 How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to enquiries and provide quotes for our cybersecurity services
  • To deliver contracted penetration testing, auditing, and consulting services
  • To send service-related communications (reports, re-test notifications)
  • To maintain records and comply with legal obligations
  • To improve our website and understand how visitors use it

We will never use your data for unsolicited marketing without explicit consent, and we will never sell your data to third parties.

04 Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract: Processing necessary to perform a contract or take pre-contractual steps.
  • Legitimate Interests: Processing necessary for our legitimate business interests, not overridden by your rights.
  • Legal Obligation: Processing required to comply with a legal obligation.
  • Consent: Where we have obtained your explicit consent.

05 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data. We may share only in these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party tools (e.g., form processing, email hosting) acting as data processors under contract.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or regulatory authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
Client Engagement DataTechnical data, credentials, or infrastructure information shared during an engagement is strictly confidential and governed by your signed engagement agreement and NDA.

06 Data Retention

  • Enquiry data: Up to 12 months from last contact if no engagement follows.
  • Client engagement data: Up to 5 years from end of engagement.
  • Financial and contractual records: 6 years, per UK tax and company law.
  • Website analytics: Aggregated and anonymised; retained indefinitely.

When data is no longer required, we securely delete or anonymise it.

07 Your Rights

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold.
  • Right to Rectification: Ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time where applicable.

Email info@eiferone.com to exercise any right. We respond within 30 days. Complaints can be lodged with the ICO.

08 Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies — no tracking or advertising cookies. We may use:

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the website to function correctly.
  • Analytics cookies: Anonymous usage data; no personally identifiable information collected.

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings.

09 Security

  • Encrypted communications (TLS/HTTPS) for all data in transit
  • Access controls and least-privilege principles for internal systems
  • Regular security reviews of our own infrastructure
  • Secure handling and destruction of sensitive client data

In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you and the ICO within 72 hours as required by law.
